WHAT IS POKÉBOT?

Pokébot automates buying Pokémon TCG products on Target, Walmart, Best Buy, and Amazon. From add-to-cart to placed order in seconds. Perfect for limited drops like ETBs and booster boxes.

⚡ CART TAB
1
Add to cart — clicks the Add to Cart button. Retries automatically if the page is slow (Retry System).
2
Close popup — dismisses the confirmation modal so it doesn't block navigation.
3
Go to checkout — jumps straight to the checkout URL, skipping the cart page entirely.
4
Save & continue — confirms your saved shipping address (toggle on to enable).
5
Place order — clicks Place Your Order. Requires a saved payment method on your account.
💰 BUDGET GUARD

Set a maximum price. The Catch It button disables if the product costs more. For scheduled and sniper runs, Pokébot still adds to cart but stops before checkout if over budget — so you stay in control.

⏰ MULTI-SCHEDULE

Add multiple schedules — each with its own URL, label, date, and time. Pokébot fires each independently when the time comes, even if the popup is closed. Great for drops happening on different sites at the same time.

🔍 POKÉMON TAB
★
Deal card — instantly shows if the current product is a Great Deal, At MSRP, Overpriced, or Scalper Price based on real Pokémon Center MSRP data.
★
Price history chart — records each price seen and shows a trend (rising/falling) so you know if the price is moving.
★
Stock Sniper — watches the current page every 2.5 seconds. The moment it comes in stock at or under MSRP, it notifies you and flashes the buy button. Enable Auto-Buy to purchase automatically. Handles reload waves (items that flicker in/out of stock).
★
Multi-URL Sniper — paste URLs for multiple products (any supported site). Pokébot checks all of them in the background every 15 seconds and triggers when any of them hit MSRP.
★
Panic Stop — immediately kills all snipers, timers, and background checks with one click.
💡 Tips: Make sure your shipping address and payment are saved before enabling Place Order. For best sniper results, navigate to the exact product page first. Multi-URL sniper works even when you're browsing other tabs.
